# UDE2222-Design-Innovation
SoleCool is a smart foot health and wellness application designed to work alongside intelligent footbeds and compression socks. The app monitors standing duration, walking activity, pressure distribution, gait patterns, and arch health to help users maintain better foot comfort and overall well-being. Through features such as stretch reminders, standing-time tracking, comfort mode therapy, and historical activity insights, SoleCool encourages healthier habits while reducing discomfort caused by prolonged standing. The system is particularly beneficial for individuals who spend long hours on their feet, such as healthcare workers, retail staff, and service personnel.
